About This Project

Welcome to my passion project! As a fervent fan of Fate/Grand Order, I wanted to create a space where I could share my enthusiasm for the game and its characters, specifically the 4* and above servants that have accompanied me through countless battles.

This website was born out of my desire to learn and master web development technologies such as Flask, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. It has been an exhilarating journey, filled with challenges and triumphs alike. By building this site from scratch, I've not only deepened my understanding of these technologies but also discovered creative ways to showcase my collection of servants.

Through this platform, I hope to connect with fellow fans and exchange insights about our favorite servants, strategies, and experiences within the game. It's a tribute to the FGO community that has been a source of inspiration and camaraderie throughout my gaming adventures.

So, take a look around, explore the servant data I've shared, and let's celebrate our shared passion for Fate/Grand Order together!
